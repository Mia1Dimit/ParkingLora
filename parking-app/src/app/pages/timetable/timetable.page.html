Timetable & Pricing

Working Hours

The working hours of the UPark will follow the ones of the University. This means that the parking lots will be open from 7:00 to 22:00 every day from Monday to Saturday.

During public holidays that are recognised by the schedule of the UoP the parkings will be closed. Also, in special days such as graduations or other events the timetable will be adapting.

Pricing

The price of the ticket, no matter the vehicle, is:
  • 1€ for Zone B
  • 1.5€ for Zone A
The final price, which is paid when you leave the parking, is affected by an extra fine if you exceed the maximum allowed duration that was shown in the initial booking. An amount of 0.5€ per 30 minutes will be continuesly adding to your current price until you free the parking spot.
